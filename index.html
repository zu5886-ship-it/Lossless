How to add a Canvas

01

Upload Video

Place your .m3u8 or .mp4 file in the Song/ or Album/ folder.

Tip: Keep file sizes small (< 5MB) for instant loading and low data use.

02

Update JSON

Add a new entry to canvas.json mapping the Song Title and Artist to your public deployment URL.

03

Enable in App

In Echo Music, open Settings, navigate to Playback Settings and toggle on Enable Canvas. Enjoy the visualizer!

Requirements: Format: .mp4 / .m3u8 • Ratio: 9:16 (Vertical) • Size: Max 5MB • Looping: 5-20s
